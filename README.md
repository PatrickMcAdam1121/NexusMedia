# NexusMedia
this a social media-esk book/comic/animation uploading website. This allows people to get their work out while also allowing them to see and give feedback on that work

In NexusMedia, you can upload a variety of files, donate to the developer(not yet working), post comments on things you enjoyed, or even add a like to posts you want to see more of

Feel free to make your own version of this and create a new branch. I would love to see where this project goes within the community. 

The only thing I ask is that you don't take full credit and give all of the amazing people who may work on this in the future credit.
